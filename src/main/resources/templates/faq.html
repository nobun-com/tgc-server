Frequently Asked Questions (FAQS)

  1. How do I make a booking?

 

You would need to sign up for an account before you are able to “Find a Class”.

Once you have an account (Hello TGC Parent!) - Simply search, select and book for the best enrichment classes. You can search for your preferred classes based on locations, age, timing and pricing filters and select classes that suit you best. Click the “BOOK NOW” button for classes with available slots and make payment instantly through Paypal or credit card. Your booking is confirmed instantly once payment is successful.

 

  1. How do I know that my class booking is successful?

 

You will get a confirmation email after each successful class booking. On the day itself, you may present the email confirmation to the education provider or simply inform the education provider that you have made a class booking through Today Got Class.

 

  1. What kind of enrichment classes can I book through Today Got Class?

 

There is a wide range of enrichment classes and activities listed on Today Got Class. Our categories range from language literacy, science and maths, brain training to sports, music and arts. Our education partners have been carefully hand-picked by us, so that you can enjoy peace of mind.

 

  1. What happens if I need to cancel a class after booking has been made?

 

Please inform us as soon as possible if you need to cancel a class after booking has been made. Generally, payments are non-refundable after a booking has been made and/or confirmed. However, we may provide either a full or partial refund depending on circumstances and taking into consideration the cancellation policy of our partners.  The decision for refund is at our sole and absolute discretion.

 

  1. How does the reward system work?

 

You will automatically receive 1 Reward Point for every S$1 spent on class bookings. You can accumulate rewards points to redeem for discounts off your next class booking. You may track the number of points collected under “My Rewards” in your Profile Page.

 

For every 100 Reward Points earned, you can redeem S$1 off your next class booking. To redeem your reward points, simply key in the number of points (in units of hundreds) that you wish to utilise while making payment for your class booking. The discount (based on number of points indicated) will be deducted from the final payment amount.

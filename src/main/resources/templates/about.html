WE SIMPLIFY PARENTHOOD

Founded by 2 Singaporean mothers with kids aged 3, 6 and 8 years old – we aim to simplify parenthood.

So, we created Today Got Class - a free-to-use service that allows parents to discover and book the best child enrichment classes.

We understand the need for quality enrichment, that's why we only work with trusted educators.

TODAY GOT CLASS IS A SERVICE PROVIDED FOR PARENTS, BY PARENTS

With over 1,000 classes and 120 centres - All educators listed on Today Got Class are personally curated by us, our family and parenting friends. We work hard to personally meet and pre-screen every educator prior to listing their classes.

As multi-tasking mothers who were once corporate warriors - we understand the scarcity of time spent with our families. That's why we simplify the booking process - find, book and get rewarded, done anytime, anywhere.

We witnessed our own kids reaping the benefits of a long term quality enrichment, which is why we invest in a term or more, seldom a class or two.

Bringing across our own experiences as parents, guided by honesty and integrity - Today Got Class aims to make it easier for parents to discover and book the best child enrichment classes.

We hope you would enjoy Today Got Class.
